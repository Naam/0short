0short let you either shorten and/or encrypt and url you want to share. For exemple let say that you want to share a direct link of a deep web resources on a non trusted network then only send the public part of the URL and separately send the key on a trusted one. Or you may simply want to shorten a long and ugly link. The crypto system is based on a AES256-CBC cipher with a random key by stretched with PBKDF2.
